About the Department

The Department of Mechanical Engineering is committed to providing quality technical education, practical training, and industry-oriented learning. The department focuses on core mechanical engineering subjects, modern manufacturing practices, design, thermal sciences, fluid mechanics, and research-based learning.

AI & ML in Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering offers an NEP-based curriculum that encourages interdisciplinary learning and skill-oriented education. The department motivates students to explore the role of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in modern engineering applications such as smart manufacturing, predictive maintenance, robotics, design optimization, thermal-fluid systems, and industrial automation.

AI for Smart Manufacturing

Application of artificial intelligence in process monitoring, quality inspection, automation, and intelligent production systems.

Machine Learning for Engineering Data

Use of machine learning techniques to analyze experimental, simulation, and industrial data for improved decision-making.

Predictive Maintenance

AI-based fault detection and condition monitoring of machines to improve reliability, safety, and performance.

Laboratories

The Department of Mechanical Engineering has well-equipped laboratories that provide students with practical exposure, experimental learning, design experience, manufacturing skills, computational training, and hands-on technical competence across core and emerging areas of mechanical engineering.

Computer Integrated Manufacturing

Provides exposure to automated manufacturing, CNC concepts, computer-controlled production systems, and modern industrial automation.

Mechatronics Lab

Introduces students to sensors, actuators, control systems, automation, robotics, and interdisciplinary mechanical-electronic systems.

Workshop

Provides hands-on training in machining, welding, fitting, casting, fabrication, and basic manufacturing processes.

Engineering Graphics & Machine Drawing

Develops engineering drawing, visualization, drafting, machine drawing, and design communication skills.
